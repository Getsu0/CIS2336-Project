    • Red Pandas:

      characteristics

      • Reddish-brown fur
      • Dark legs
      • Whie Ears
      • Tail with rings

        • In India, China, and Nepal, red pandas are officially protected due to their endangered status.

        Their main risks include poaching, human meddling, and habitat loss and degradation.

      • Tree Pangolins:

        characteristics

        • Keratin Scales Cover 90% Of Their Body
        • Short Hands & Feet
        • Long Noses
        • Claws Are Curved

          • As found in mid-Africa, they live in forests. They can roll up into a ball when they feel threatened. With only using its scales to defend itself.

          Their main risks include Black Market trafficing, used as medicine, and poaching..

        • Fennec Foxes:

          characteristics

          • Sand-color fur
          • Bright legs
          • White Ears
          • Red Stripes On Back

            • The fennec fox can be found all over the Sahara,They live in little sand dunes as well as large, treeless sand areas with sparse flora like grasses, sedges, and small bushes.

            The fennec fox was frequently trapped and sold for visitors. As a result, they disappeared from these places and was confined to outlying locations like in southern Morocco.
