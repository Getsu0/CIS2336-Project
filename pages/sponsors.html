Sponsors

​​NASSA

Our main sponsor for animal wildlife rescue is NASSA, who is also our biggest supporter. Their dedication to our conservation efforts is unwavering, and they have played a crucial role in the protection and preservation of endangered species. Through their generous financial assistance and expert guidance, NASSA has enabled us to make a significant difference in the world and create a better future for wildlife. We are deeply grateful for their ongoing support and unwavering commitment to our cause, and we look forward to continuing our partnership to safeguard and preserve animal habitats.

Jeper

Jeper is a jeep company with a strong interest in animal wildlife rescue and is committed to making a positive impact on the world by supporting conservation efforts. They sponsor various wildlife rescue programs and design innovative off-road vehicles equipped with features to navigate challenging terrains. Jeper's commitment to animal welfare is a vital part of their social responsibility program, and they're involved in various environmental initiatives that promote sustainability and eco-friendliness.

Dont Duns Donuts

Dont Duns Donuts is more than just a donut shop; they are committed to supporting animal wildlife rescue and contributing to conservation efforts. By sponsoring various wildlife rescue programs, they aim to protect and preserve endangered species. Dont Duns Donuts believes that it's essential for individuals to play a role in protecting the environment and wildlife, and their social responsibility program reflects this belief. They take pride in partnering with animal welfare organizations to create a positive impact on the world, making sure that they are doing their part in making a difference.

Pitza House

Pitza House is a pizza shop that cares about animal wildlife rescue and wants to help make the world a better place. They support wildlife rescue programs and work hard to protect endangered animals. Pitza House thinks it's important to help animal welfare groups and take care of the environment. They have a program where they partner with organizations who share their values to help make a positive impact on the world. Pitza House is a great example of a business that wants to make a difference and help animals in need.
